My Services

Blender

Three years of experience using Blender for 3D modelling, texturing, and basic animation. Proficient in sculpting tools, mesh editing, material creation, and rendering with Eevee and Cycles. Ability to develop stylised and realistic assets for video games, visualisations, or personal projects. Familiarity with simple rigging, physical simulations (such as particles and fluids), and video editing within the Blender environment. Constantly evolving technically, with an interest in improving skills in advanced animation, procedural shading, and compositing.

Unity

Developer with solid experience in the Unity environment, specialising in the creation of video games and interactive simulations. For two years, I have worked on projects ranging from prototypes to functional products, applying knowledge in C# programming, game mechanics design, physics implementation, animations, and performance optimisation. Familiarity with particle systems, lighting, UI/UX, and asset management. Ability to work in collaborative environments with version control (Git) and a focus on creative problem-solving for technical issues.

Unreal

Practical knowledge of Unreal Engine acquired over two years, focused primarily on creating interactive environments and video game prototypes. Familiarity with the Blueprints system for implementing basic game logic, simple animations, and visual effects. Ability to work with standard materials, lighting, and physics. Experience in project organisation, asset import, and general adjustments to improve visual presentation. Constantly learning new tools within the engine, with an interest in further developing skills in C++ programming and more complex level design.

Adobe Photoshop

Three years of experience using Adobe Photoshop for image editing, graphic design, and visual content creation. Proficient in basic tools such as layers, masks, colour adjustments, cropping, and retouching. Ability to develop visual compositions for social media, presentations, and personal projects. Familiarity with typography, simple effects, and optimised exporting for different formats. Constantly learning new techniques, with an interest in improving skills in photomontage, advanced manipulation, and professional digital design.

Adobe Premiere

Two years of experience using Adobe Premiere Pro for video editing in personal and collaborative projects. Proficient in basic tools such as cuts, transitions, colour correction, audio editing, and the use of keyframes for simple animations. Ability to structure visual narratives, integrate graphics, and export optimised content for different platforms (YouTube, social media, presentations). Familiarity with sequence organisation, use of preset effects, and audio synchronisation. Constantly learning advanced techniques such as multi-camera, masks, and dynamic editing.

Adobe Substance Painter 3D

Three years of experience using Adobe Substance 3D Painter for texturing 3D models in personal and collaborative projects. Proficient in PBR materials, layers, masks, and custom brushes to achieve realistic and stylised finishes. Familiarity with baking maps (normal, AO, curvature, etc.) and integrating textures into engines such as Unity and Unreal Engine. Ability to organise efficient workflows, adjust material parameters, and export optimised textures for different environments. Constantly learning advanced techniques such as smart materials, wear effects, and process automation.
